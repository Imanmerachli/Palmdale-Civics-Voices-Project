Our Mission

Our mission is to connect youth with local government by sharing real stories from Palmdale’s civic leaders. Through interviews and reflections, we aim to inspire the next generation to understand, engage with, and shape their community.

About Us

The Palmdale Civic Voices Project is a youth-led initiative that highlights the stories of local leaders through interviews and articles. Our goal is to make civic life more accessible, relatable, and inspiring for young people in our community.
